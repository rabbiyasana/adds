Gold Ad Pack

How It Works

1. Registration

In order to participate in the program you should Register an account and verify your email. After registering you will receive an email with a verification link that you should click in order to get your account verified. Once verified you will be granted access to the OneAdPack member area.

2. Purchasing AddPack

Simply click on the "Purchase" Adpack button to make a purchase. The process is straightforward, just decide how many adpack you want and follow instructions.

3. Minimum and Maximum Purchase

Currenlty, the minimum Active AdPack purchase is 1 ($1.00) and the maximum is 5000. The maximum daily purchase is 500 AdPacks.

4. Earning by Visiting Websites

You earn 0.4333% of your spend for every visit that you made and up to 4.333% every day (with 10 visits) for 30 days. Visiting more than 10 sites will not add any additional monetary rewards but will increase your advertising credits.

5. Repurchasing AdPacks

As soon as you have more than $1.00 in commissions you will be able to repurchase more AdPacks. Additionally, in order to increase the stability of the program 20% of your daily earnings and commissions will be put in a repurchase wallet that you can only use to repurchase AdPacks.

6. Advertising

When you join you are automatically given 1 Advertising Slot that you can use to add, edit or remove your sites that will be shown on the website rotation as fairly as possible. You will be given 100 credits for every dollar spent. You should allocate credits to your website(s) in order to show them on the OneAdPack rotation. Whenever you run out of credits your website(s) stop showing up.

7. Affiliate Commissions

We offer a two tiers Affiliate programs. Your direct referrals will earn you 7% of their purchases made with a payment processor and 3% of their repurchases. Second tier referrals (referrals of your referrals) will earn you 3% of their purchases made with a payment processor and 1% of their repurchases.

8. Withdrawals

When you reach the treshold of the minimum required to cashout you will be allowed to request a withdraw from the Withdrawal page. We pay out with the "as soon as possible" formula, we have no reason to keep the money in our accounts but we pay only 1 payout request per person at a time!
